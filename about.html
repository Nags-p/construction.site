Founder

Pavan Kumar K P

Founder and Managing Director

Pavan Kumar K P established Shaydri Constructions and Developers with a vision to transform the construction and real estate landscape. With years of experience in architecture, planning, and project management, he has led the company to achieve milestones in quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. His commitment to sustainable development and modern building practices sets Shaydri Constructions apart in the industry.

Pavan’s leadership emphasizes precision, collaboration, and adaptability, ensuring each project meets the unique needs of the clients while maintaining the highest standards. His expertise has been instrumental in delivering iconic projects across various domains, including real estate development, material contracting, and interior design.

Outside of his professional endeavors, Pavan is an avid advocate for innovative solutions and believes in fostering growth and partnerships to build a brighter future for communities.
